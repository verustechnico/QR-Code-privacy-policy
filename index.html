Privacy Policy

QRXpress is committed to protect your privacy. This application does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. All features are designed to function without gathering any data from you.

1. Permissions Used

QRXpress requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: To scan QR codes and barcodes using your device's camera.
  • Gallery: To pick an image from your device to scan QR codes or barcodes from an image.

These permissions are used solely for their stated purpose and no data is transmitted or stored remotely.

2. Local Data Storage

The app stores your scan history and generated QR codes locally on your device. No data is uploaded to any server or external system. You have full control over your data.

3. No Data Collection

We do not collect, monitor, or transmit any user data. QRXpress functions entirely offline, apart from system-level permissions used for functionality.
